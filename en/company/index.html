Established in 2000, Polylog Consulting Group specializes in PR and integrated marketing campaigns. Our services include PR, event management, GR, and corporate media.

Dozens of PR consultants, managers and thought leaders
work for the client

Team

Polylog is a professional team with education and experience in fields such as journalism, economics, sociology, psychology, history.

Experts and celebrities

Opinion leaders are involved in the work on projects. Experts in key areas of business. Television, radio, cinema and music celebrities. Officials.

Travel agency

We have our own travel agency Corporate Travel. It allows us to organize international events of any complexity.

Together for a long time

  • We emphasize long-term relationships, not one-off projects; 90% of our clients are regular customers.
  • We are a consulting group, not just an agency; our work is based on a thorough understanding of our clients’ needs and ranges from designing creative solutions to organizing campaigns and projects of any degree of complexity.
  • „Polylog” is the only Russian PR agency certified according to ISO 9001:2015. Areas are development and implementation of public relations programs and processes related to the organization of business events.
  • We achieve results, keeping track of client’s KPIs.
