The new automated system for the Federal Agency for Tourism

In December 2017, the Research and Development Department of the Polylog Consulting Group successfully completed project on automating the processes of issuing permits by the Federal Agency for Tourism to carry out activities in the field of tourism related to the use of foreign tourist vessels (further — Permits).

In December 2017, the Research and Development Department of the Polylog Consulting Group successfully completed project on automating the processes of issuing permits by the Federal Agency for Tourism to carry out activities in the field of tourism related to the use of foreign tourist vessels (further — Permits).

In accordance with the Decree of the Government of the Russian Federation No. 679 of June 03, 2017, the Federal Agency for Tourism is determined by the authorized body to issue Permits.

The purpose of the project was to automate the entire cycle of processing applications for the provision of public services. According to the results of the first stage of the project (survey and projecting), a package of technical documentation was formed, including a draft passport of public services, recommendations for optimizing the processes of providing public services, as well as a draft of technical specification for the placement of services on Public Services Portal of the Russian Federation

At the second stage, the development of an Automated information system “issuance of permits for activities in the field of tourism related to the use of foreign tourist vessels” was carried out directly. The system provides a complete cycle of processing of applications by the Federal Agency for Tourism: from the moment of receipt of an application for the provision of public services to the issuance of a Permit to the applicant.
