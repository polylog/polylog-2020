In December 2016 Research & Development Department of the Polylog Consulting Group successfully realized the project on development of the Unified Federal Register of Tour operators which is used for the provision of the public service of the Federal Tourism Agency of Russia.

Система «Единый федеральный реестр туроператоров» позволяет сотрудникам Ростуризма осуществлять комплексную обработку запросов на предоставление государственной услуги, поданной на бумаге или через Единый портал государственных и муниципальных услуг.

Unified Federal Register of Tour operators allows employees of the Federal Tourism Agency of Russia to provide complex request processing for e-service provision. The necessity to develop the system is attributable to alternations to the Federal law № 132 that came into effect on the 1st of January 2017 and extended requirements to Tour operators.

We assisted the Federal Tourism Agency of Russia in integration into the interdepartmental electronic interaction system (IEIS)

Polylog Consulting Group analyzed legislations and developed the system expanding data contained in the register and improved its functioning. We assisted the Federal Tourism Agency of Russia in integration into the interdepartmental electronic interaction system (IEIS) as well.

In order to increase efficiency of the IEIS functioning we integrated a screening subsystem into that secured control of the system’s functioning.
