The Eighth International Diana Association Dealer Conference was held on December, 1 in Moscow. It was timed to coincide with the 20th anniversary of Diana Association, one of the leading companies in the market of construction materials. The conference was organized by “Polylog” Consulting Group.

Our scope of functions included

  • preparatory activities
  • technical support of the conference
  • interaction with participants and media
  • initiation of publications
  • design development and design of the venue
  • professional photography during the conference
  • etc.

Diana Association entered the Russian market in 1991. Within a framework of the Eighth International Conference dedicated to the 20th anniversary of the Association, special attention was paid to the construction of an innovative factory for production of roofing and waterproofing materials in the Saratov region.

Minister of Investment Policy of the Saratov region Irina Blokhina and Deputy Prime Minister of the Saratov region Alexei Zhandarov taking part in the conference underlined socio-economic importance of building a factory for the Saratov region. Talking about cost reduction of construction materials, Alexander Zhandarov pointed out that “the factory will give employment to 180 local residents and provide them with respectable salary. Highly skilled university graduates will be able to work there thanks to fully automated equipment.

The conference brought together more than 100 people from all over Russia and foreign countries. Russian and foreign representatives of the construction business exchanged views on major trends in such business areas as distribution of construction materials, production, commercial real estate management and logistics. They could also have an informal talk with colleagues and representatives of specialized and business media covering the event.
