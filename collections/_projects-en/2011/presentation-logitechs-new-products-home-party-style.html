The presentation of Logitech’s new products was held on October, 11 in Moscow. Logithech is a global provider of personal peripherals for computers and other digital platforms. The event was organized by “Polylog" CG.

Logitech

Logitech is the worldwide leader in the production of peripheral devices that provide its users with the opportunity to get immersed themselves in the digital entertainment world. Logitech’s gadgets are compatible with different platforms. All audio- and video entertainment devices, games, social networking, data transfer over the Internet, video security system and home theater system control are easily implemented. Logitech International S.A., founded in 1981 in Switzerland, is listed on the Nasdaq Global Select Market under the symbol LOGI and on the SIX Swiss Exchange under the symbol LOGN.

The guests could find out more about Logitech’s new wireless devices, have a look at new interesting gadgets and get the answers to all the questions from the company’s representatives. Logitech presented its unique technological solutions, for example, the world's first Wireless Solar Keyboard K750, which is recharged by sunlight or artificial light and it works in total darkness up to three months (depending on the conditions of usage) or Wireless Headset H600. Logitech’s cooling Touch Lapdesk N600 let the guests get cozy on the sofa and read the latest news on the laptop.

Special testing area was prepared for the gamers. The guests could try out Logitech G27 Racing Wheel, Driving Force GT and Playseat. Besides, they immersed themselves in the game and absolutely enjoyed the gameplay using Logitech Wireless Gaming Mouse G700 with 13 programmable controls. The surround sound of 7.1 Logitech Wireless Gaming Headset G930 and the amazing sound quality aroused new strong positive emotions even in the most avid gamers.

Logitech Colour Collection of new computer accessories was presented. It included a wide range of webcams and mice with sumptuous color design primarily aimed at women.

At the end of the official presentation the guests were invited to participate in sushi and cocktail-making workshops, as well as explore novelties of the company in special testing areas. Each room was decorated by Logitech's equipment, thereby creating an authentic image of well-thought-out digital home. The employees from Logitech’s Russian and Swiss offices shared their exclusive information about technical and design solutions with Russia’s leading IT Media.
