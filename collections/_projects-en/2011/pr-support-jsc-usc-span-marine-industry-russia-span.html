“Polylog” Consulting Group provided PR-support to JSC “United Shipbuilding Corporation” (USC) in the IInd International Forum “Marine Industry of Russia” which was held on May, 18-20 in Moscow.

“Polylog” Consulting Group’s project team organized the process of informing the media on the “United Shipbuilding Corporation’s” participation in the Forum and provided the invitation of journalists to the official events with representatives of the Corporation.

The representatives of Russian mass media could meet and interviewed executives of the Corporation on the first day of the Forum. As a result, more than 50 media messages including Television and Radio were initiated.
