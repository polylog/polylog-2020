EMC FORUM is an important event in the IT world and the place where technologies, business, and science meet. It is attended by heads of IT companies, IT directors, and IT heads of companies of absolutely different industries. Polylog provided PR support of the event.

1,104 Participants

The forum featured a business program and an exhibition. Adrian McDonald, President EMC EMEA, and representatives of leading business partners of the corporation participated in the plenary discussion. The exhibition featured such large IT corporations as Cisco, Intel, Lenovo, VMware, Brocade, and many more. The total number of the participants was 1,104.

The forum also provided a free certification for IT specialists, a separate section for information security heads, and a TV studio where anyone could leave their wishes and impressions on video.
