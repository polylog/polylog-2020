August 18th, 2013: Apps4All Company and “Polylog” consulting group carried out a summer conference for the participants of the Russian market of mobile applications developers Apps4All Summer Fest. The venue was the spacious three -deck motor ship “Gzhel”, the event lasted for 9.5 hours.

For the first time in the history of the Russian event-industry representatives of the major players in the domestic market of mobile applications were able to talk business in an atmosphere of Sunday cocktail on the Moscow River and enjoy a 45-pound piece of “Korkunov” cake. The dessert menu is not surprising: businessman Andrei Korkunov owns 15 % of the Apps4All Company.

The organizers of the conference were Apps4All and “Polylog” Consulting that has been Apps4All’s partner since 2011 — at the Forum it provided complete cycle of technical work on the organization of the event, the Agency also worked with the participants, along with preparation of information materials, media relations and promotion of the event in the Internet and off-line information resources.

300 experts and developers

The conference was attended by more than 300 leading IT— experts and developers, including Huawei (General strategic partner ), Google (Partner of the Forum ), Fabrus (Analytical Partner), The Department of Information Technologies of the city of Moscow, Softline, MegaLabs, Mail.Ru Group, Analytical Center Video International, GALS Soft , Playtestix, Altarix, Mobio, WapStart, Mobile info, J’son & Partners Consulting, InfoShell, OPPO. The event gathered more than 30 leading Russian federal and industry media, including RBC Daily, Metro, “Trud”, Computer Build magazine and others.

“The largest number of participants gathered the section “Prospects for cooperation of mobile operators and developers” — the joint session of Huawei and MegaLabs; it was attended by representatives of the “Big Three”. We even had to extend the debate timing— the developers had a lot of questions for the largest Russian mobile operators. In my opinion, the most important outcome of this debate — is the “Big Three” feedback to the participants of the market of mobile applications, which expressed the readiness of mobile operators to review and analyze investment ideas of developers”— said Zamir Shukhov, the Head of the Event department, “Polylog” CG.

In 2013 “Polylog” consulting group successfully completed a number of other projects to support young entrepreneurs, innovations and IT— education. In March — April took place an unprecedented for innovations and venture capital investments market Road Show of Russian development institutes — Russian StartUp Tour. In August was completed a project for creation of a network of regional youth centers to support innovations City Startup Club, which joined start-up teams, entrepreneurs, business angels, mentors, experts and venture capitalists in 15 regions of Russia .

Apps4All Company

Founded in 2011 by young entrepreneurs Alexander Vasilyev and Sergei Borisov. The company’s mission is in development and organization of the only Russian ecosystem of mobile developments that combines on their online and offline venues all market participants: developers, the largest IT— corporations, start-up projects, venture capital investors, businesses and government agencies. Over 1,000 companies and developers are more than 4700 mobile applications that they’ve created are registered at the moment at the apps4all.ru portal.
