On June 3-6, Moscow City Government (represented by the Foreign Trade and International Relations Department) for the first time participated in 2014 AUTOMATICA Automation and Mechatronics Trade Fair in Munich, Germany. The main goal of the event was to attract new international partners and investors to projects in Moscow. The city was represented by various institutions of the Russian Academy of Sciences (RAS) and its partner companies.

Polylog Consulting Group was a technical operator of the event. The company executed the following scope of works:

  • Booth construction and furnishing
  • Catering
  • Logistics
  • Designing and manufacturing promotional merchandise and printed products etc.

8 Co-Exhibitors

Eight co-exhibitors worked at the booth, including Space Research Institute (RAS), the Institute of Biomedical Problems (RAS), the Research and Technology Center of Unique Instrumentation (RAS), the Development Design Office of Oceanological Engineering (RAS), NPO Android Technics etc.

Additionally, Polylog Consulting Group supervised the co-exhibitors' work and formed the business agenda.

The following officials were present as honorary guests: Eugene Dridze, the head of official delegation, deputy head of Moscow Foreign Trade and International Relations Department; Andrey Grozov, the Consul General of the Russian Federation in Munich; Martin Grossman, the head of International Trade Department of Bavaria, and his colleague Claudia Schleicher; Bernd Pantze, the official representative of Messe Munchen International board of directors for cooperation with the Russian Federation.
